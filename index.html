About SysMoBench

SysMoBench provides a comprehensive suite of benchmarks designed to evaluate and compare system-level modeling approaches. Our goal is to establish standardized metrics and reproducible evaluation methodologies for the community.

Key Features

📊

Comprehensive Metrics

Standardized evaluation metrics covering accuracy, efficiency, and scalability.

🔄

Reproducible

Fully reproducible benchmarks with documented configurations and datasets.

🔧

Extensible

Easy to add new benchmarks, models, and evaluation criteria.
